Naciones Celtas II Link to heading

Compilation consisting of three CDs of Folk music from the following regions and countries:
Galicia, Asturias, Andalusia, Scotland, Ireland, Cornwall, Wales, Isle of Man, Brittany, Aragon, Canary Islands, Cantabria, Valencian Community, Castile and León, Catalonia, Madrid, Basque Country, and Portugal.

This album contains a total of 46 recordings by different artists.

  • Second CD, track 16: Alameda das Burgas (Alberto Conde)
