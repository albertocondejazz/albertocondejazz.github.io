Corriente del Golfo Link to heading

“La impresión ahora es la de haberse embarcado en su expedición más aventurera y significativa, y el hecho mismo de haberle dedicado siete años de desvelos lo acredita. Mucho que leer, aprender y escuchar en esta singladura de vocación marinera pero muy altos vuelos….”
— Fernando Neira

HE - Human Evolution Music Project Link to heading

Human Evolution is an unprecedented musical project within the so-called World Music movement. It is the result of the fusion of Iberian-origin music with traditional Indian music, taking jazz as the connecting link. Piano, sitar, Indian tabla, tenor saxophone, flute, bagpipe, viola da gamba, and electronic music generate a unique combination in a constant, suggestive, and exciting evolution, inspired by the history of human evolution.

The goal of this ensemble is to maintain a distinctive sound within the international jazz scene, inspired by both North American and Iberian musical traditions.

Andaina Link to heading

A new musical work by Alberto Conde Trio in which, once again, Galician music travels through Jazz with new compositions by Alberto Conde himself.

In addition to the trio—featuring double bassist Baldo Martínez, drummer-percussionist Nirankar Khalsa, and Alberto Conde—this new project includes special collaborations with two renowned guest artists: flamenco-Galician guitarist Cuchús Pimentel and the outstanding Galician voice of Maite Dono, both of whom undoubtedly contribute meaningful depth and a distinctive sound to the whole work.

Tracks Link to heading

  1. Trobador
  2. Amoriños collín
  3. Alalá das mariñas
  4. Arepita con jamón
  5. Esencia
  6. Mestizo
  7. Andaina
  8. Astrolabio
  9. Teño saudade
  10. Time remembered

The leitmotif of the album is based on the inevitable changes that human beings are both willing to face and exposed to throughout life, told through a musical journey from Galicia to Latin America—a one-way voyage.
‘A journey of generational evolution’, portraying how the grandchildren or great-grandchildren of the first Galician and Spanish emigrants will be the ones to discover the land of their ancestors, when their grandparents were never able to return. Grandchildren who are mostly the children of unions between Spaniards and Creoles, Afro-Americans, Italians, Indigenous people, etc.

Andaina is a musical journey through time into Galician roots, where jazz serves as the vehicle that connects the eras, and the melodies reflect the traveler’s many situations.

Entremares Link to heading

Entremares brings together the experiences of Alberto Conde and Baldo Martínez in fusing jazz with Galician music. Supported by Nirankar Khalsa (Afro-American percussionist-drummer), they achieve a fresh and original sound. Rather than just Galician Music, we should speak of World Music with Galician roots.

This ensemble creates an open atmosphere with simple melodies, spontaneity, expressiveness, and rhythmic variety: Galician music enriched by Afro-American music.

A Lagoa Dos Atlantes Link to heading

It is a record that combines Jazz, Classical, and Baroque with Galician Music, featuring prominent Galician artists of Folk and Traditional Music. It received awards at the Lorient Festival (breakthrough group), the New Music Award on the TVG program Luar, and best avant-garde Galician album in Faro de Vigo and Ideal Gallego. The program Diálogos 3 by RNE, voiced by its director Ramón Trecet, described it as “the birth of Muiñeirajazz.”
